Hike Info

Description

The May Lake / Mt. Hoffman hike is moderate to strenuous hike that follows the pack train trail to May Lake for the first mile plus of the trail. May Lake is a High Sierra Lake and camp located at the base of the nearly 11,000-foot Mt. Hoffman. Since it is a High Sierra Camp the trail is well marked and maintained.

It is worth noting that much of this hike is above timberline and very exposed. Care should be taken to protect yourself from the sun and bring plenty of water. And as always be mindful of changing weather conditions as thunderstorms can form quickly in the High Sierra

What I Love About This Hike

There is so much to love about this from the sparkling brilliance of May Lake to the amazing views from the summit of Mt. Hoffman.

The cold refreshing waters of May Lake offer a welcome respite before of after a summiting of Mt. Hoffman.
