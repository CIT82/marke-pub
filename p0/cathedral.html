Hike Info

Description

While you do climb nearly 1500 feet in elevation in this hike it is made easier by a couple sets of switch backs. The first set is long and gradual while the second set are short and steep.

This trail now requires a permit. Which can be obtained through the nps.gov site. This was not the case when I hike this back in 2013

If you are afraid of heights it is worth noting that the last portion of this hike traverses ledges that are at some points less than two feet across. There is a cable anchored to the rock to hold onto

What I Love About This Hike

The adventurous nature of this hike is really fun. There is something about being a foot or two from the edge looking 1500 feet down.

The view the surrounding red rock wall, and the green down below along the Virgin river is amazing
