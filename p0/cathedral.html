Hike Info

Description

The Cathedral Lakes Trail in Yosemite National Park offers a moderately challenging hike that rewards adventurers with stunning alpine lake vistas and expansive mountain views.

I can't speak to the traditional trail to these lakes since I have never been on it. My family has been taking a different route to these lakes since I have been alive. We take the Medicott Dome access trail which is not as well maintained but saves some miles. One cool thing about this way is that it has a great vista point for Tenaya Lake.

What I Love About This Hike

Fantastic views of Cathedral and Echo Peaks. Along with great swimming.

This must do hike for me when I am camping it Tuolumne Meadows. The campground has been closed for the past couple of years, and I miss this place.
