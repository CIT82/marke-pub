Park Info

General Park Information

Yosemite National Park, situated in California’s Sierra Nevada mountains, is renowned for its stunning granite cliffs, ancient sequoias, and diverse ecosystems. Established in 1890, it spans nearly 1,200 square miles and attracts millions of visitors annually.

Home to some iconic landmarks like Half Dome and El Capitan

Yosemite Valley's protected status pre-dates the National Park system. It along with Mariposa Grove were given to the state of California In the Yosemite Grant Act by Abraham Lincoln in 1864. The act required the land by set aside for public use.

What I Love About This Park
