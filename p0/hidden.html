Hike Info

Description

Hidden Lake is a picturesque alpine lake situated in Glacier National Park, Montana, renowned for its crystal-clear waters and breathtaking mountain scenery. The lake is nestled at an elevation of approximately 6,375 feet (1,943 meters) and is encircled by prominent peaks, including Bearhat Mountain to the southwest, Dragons Tail to the south, Clements Mountain to the north, and Reynolds Mountain to the east.

What I Love About This Hike

The peaks surrounding Logan pass offer an amazing back drop for this hike. When I went the trail down to the lake from the overlook was closed but still this is a must do hike while in Glacier.
